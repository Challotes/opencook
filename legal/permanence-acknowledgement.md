# BSVibes — Permanence Acknowledgement (DRAFT)

> **This is a working draft for attorney review. It is not legal advice and is not yet in force.**
> This short notice is shown to a user **before their first post**, as an affirmative-consent gate.

## Notice text (shown before the first post)

Your posts are written to a public blockchain — they are **permanent and visible to anyone, forever**, and **we cannot delete them**. If you lose your recovery file or forget your passphrase, your identity and any value tied to it are **lost for good, with no way to recover them**. Please **don't post your real name, contact details, or anything private** — once it's on-chain, it stays on-chain. By continuing, you accept this.
